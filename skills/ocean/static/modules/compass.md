# Compass Module Contract

Active module: Compass

Purpose: convert evidence boundaries into research route, experiment design, idea prioritization, and submission strategy.

## Required artifact

- Evidence-based Idea Card
- Experiment Plan
- Strategy Route
- Stop Condition for Unsupported Ideas

## Rules

- Do not write a polished abstract, title, or journal pitch before claim-evidence readiness is established.
- Do not recommend a journal tier as if submission-ready without data, methods, validation, and contribution clarity.
- Separate "promising idea" from "publishable result".
- Convert overstrong user requests into readiness gates and next experiments.

## Research route fields

| Decision | Current evidence | Required next evidence | Action | Stop condition |
|---|---|---|---|---|

Compass may hand off to nature-style writing only after OCEAN verifies which claims are safe to write.

## Stop when

Stop before journal positioning, polished abstracts, or submission strategy if the evidence boundary cannot support the core claim or if the next required evidence is undefined.
