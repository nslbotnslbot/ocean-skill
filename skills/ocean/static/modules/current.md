# Current Module Contract

Active module: Current

Purpose: assess field momentum, direction flow, consensus-vs-hype, and recent research positioning from a traceable source corpus.

## Required artifact

- Trend Map
- Direction-flow Boundary
- Consensus-vs-Hype Notes
- Missing-search Boundary

## Rules

- Do not call a direction "hot", "accepted", "mature", or "settled" without a search corpus, date range, inclusion criteria, and inspected sources.
- Distinguish field existence from field consensus.
- Distinguish publication volume from validated scientific maturity.
- If no literature/source corpus is provided, produce a search plan and stop before trend claims.

## Safe outputs

- "This is a plausible direction to search."
- "The current packet cannot establish trend strength."
- "A trend claim would require PubMed/OpenAlex/preprint queries, date filters, and source triage."

## Stop when

Stop before trend, consensus, maturity, or hype claims if no source corpus, date range, inclusion criteria, and inspected sources are available.
